Mission

The Mission of the Seventh-day Adventist Church
is to proclaim to all the people
the Everlasting Gospel in the context of the
Three Angels Message of Revelation 14:6-12 leading them to accept Jesus as personal Saviour and to unite with His church, and nurturing them in preparation for His soon return.
